THE LOST CITY OF ATLANTIS

Rich in resources and presence of highly intellectual minds, the city of Atlantis developed rapidly.
Established as the most advanced city in the world way ahead of its time, the city mysteriously disappeared in the ocean not to be found.
As the rest of the world developed relatively slowly over time, the reason for the demise of the city of Atlantis is getting clearer.

With the advancement, we are exploiting more and more resources such as trees and minerals.
The scarcity of resources which seemed impossible is now the cause for the modern world to begin collapsing into the sea.

Our hero is in the world and is trying to find a way to save the world.
Your job is to interact with the world using our hero and make his misssion possible.

LET'S SEE IF THE WORLD CAN BE SAVED!!!
